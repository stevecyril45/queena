About Us

Since our incorporation in 2008, Queena Investment Limited has grown into a trusted name in the Nigerian logistics and maritime industry. We are a bonded terminal operator approved by the Nigeria Customs Service, providing reliable services in shipping and clearing, haulage, air freight, warehousing, and consulting.

Our mission is simple: to simplify and accelerate cargo clearance, ensuring that every delivery is handled with efficiency, safety, and transparency. Backed by a modern fleet, secure warehouse facilities, and a highly experienced team, we deliver end-to-end logistics solutions tailored to the needs of businesses both locally and internationally.

Guided by our core values of Integrity, Customer Focus, Innovation, Safety, and Efficiency, we remain committed to building long-term partnerships and driving growth across Africa and beyond.
